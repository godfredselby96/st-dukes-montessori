About Us

At St. Dukes Montessori, we provide Montessori-inspired learning for early years and a strong, well-rounded curriculum for Elementary and Junior High levels. Our experienced teachers focus on academic excellence, creativity, technology skills, and whole-child development in a safe and nurturing environment. Students are encouraged to explore, learn, and grow, preparing them for future success in school and life.

Our Vision

For all children to reach their full potential as collaborative and independent learners by providing a Montessori education that nurtures their spirit as they become engaged citixens of the World

Our Mission

To produce future leaders who are Determined, Generous, Sincere and Capable

Our Approach

Personalized, activity-based learning rooted in Montessori principles, fostering character, problem-solving, and life skills.

Achievements

Learners excel in academics, UCMAS competitions, sports, creative arts, and computing, developing skills for future success.
